About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research

This portfolio serves as the capstone for the "Generative AI for Work & Research" course. The program explored the transformative potential of Large Language Models and AI tools in streamlining complex workflows, enhancing data analysis, and driving innovation.

By integrating AI capabilities—such as automated data interpretation, rapid prototyping with tools like Cursor, and strategic problem-solving—this course has empowered me to conceptualize and develop robust digital health solutions and analytical reports that address real-world planetary and human health challenges.
